  • Welcome to Canvas Quest! It looks like this is the first time you have visited here, so let's cover some basics.

    Controls Arrow keys to move around. Hit "esc" key to bring up load/save dialog. Hit "spacebar" to reload the game (equivalent to a browser reload).

    Story Your name is Foobar the Bazbarian. The young Princess Wynn has fallen ill with a mysterious disease. You have been chosen by your kingdom to travel to the Dungeon of Doom to retrieve the Magic Canvas. The Magic Canvas has the amazing ability to bring to reality whatever is painted on it. The King has found the world's greatest artist - Leonardo - to paint the Princess Wynn in complete health. Your job is to brave the defenses of the Dungeon of Doom and return the Canvas back to the King.

    GamePlay You must brave deeper and deeper levels, fight monsters, solve puzzles, and eventually win the day. This game is turn based, so nothing will happen until you move. Also note that there is a Score counter that goes down for every move you make - so try to make every move count! To fight a monster, just run into it. You will find gold, food, and occasionally a trader who can help you on your journey. Good luck!
